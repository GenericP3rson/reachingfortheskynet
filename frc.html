We Are a Part of FIRST® Robotics Competition

FIRST® Robotics Competition challenges high-school students (under the guidance of a volunteer professional) to raise funds, construct a team "brand", and design, build, and program an industrial-sized robot, to compete in a field-game with strict rules and various tasks. It allows students to experience and participate in complex engineering processes, develop authentic business and marketing skills, and promote the growth of leadership, communication, and other team skills invaluable in the workplace and everyday life.

What is FIRST®?

FIRST® is a prominent non-profit public charity that designs accessible programs to empower young people to pursue education and careers in science, technology, engineering, and math and also build personal skills to become leaders and innovators in the workplace. It stimulates the growth of these skills by engaging students in menter-based programs. FIRST® has huge impact, creating a huge global community of people passionate about STEM, with over 680,000 students, ranging from Pre-K to 12th grade.

"The mission of FIRST® is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership."

Safety First.

Our team's safety is our first priority. The resources below shaped our standards and rules for safety.
