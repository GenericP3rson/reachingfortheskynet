Our Mission

Through our years in high school, we wanted to challenge ourselves with a higher level of robotics and engineering. We strive to enhance Keystone's current engineering program, opening new engineering opportunities in our school and community and creating a diverse community of student engineers. By introducing the FIRST Robotics Program, we hope to open new STEM opportunities for high school students in our school, then broaden that impact in our community.

As a Team, We Strive To...

1. Enhance the Engineering Programs in our School and Community

2. Provide Opportunities for Students to Obtain Real-World Engineering Skills and Experience

3. Create and Foster a Community of Student Engineers
