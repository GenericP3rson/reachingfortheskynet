Who are we?

We are Keystone School's FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) Team, Reaching for the Skynet (RfS). We are a student-lead team of high schoolers based in San Antonio, Texas.


What is FRC?


FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an annual competition during which high students from around the world face off in 3v3 robotics competitions. Its goals is to spread STEM by making it exciting!

Our Mission


Reaching for the Skynet (RfS) is Keystone School's FIRST Robotics Team. Started by the Class of 2022 in 2020, RfS strives to teach students real-world engineering skills through designing, building, and programming a robot for FRC.

How Can You Help?


The entire FRC experience costs roughly $10,000 for registration, robot parts, tools, and travel. RfS is open to both monetary and in-kind sponsorships.
